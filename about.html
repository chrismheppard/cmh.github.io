The Man, The Myth, The (Lack of a) Plan

CyberSolutionsOhio is the brainchild of Chris Heppard, a seasoned professional with a deep understanding of the digital landscape and a refreshing lack of a formal, five-year plan. This isn't a weakness; it's a strategic advantage. In a world of rigid, outdated business models, Chris embraces agility, adapting to the ever-changing needs of his clients with a flexibility that larger firms can only dream of.

Our mission is to serve humans. We cut through the jargon and deliver real-world solutions that are both powerful and intuitive. We believe that technology should empower, not intimidate, and we're committed to building digital experiences that are accessible to everyone.
